# 🌍 Project Story

Every business generates thousands of sales transactions daily, but raw numbers alone cannot answer critical business questions.

Companies need to understand:

- Which products generate the most revenue?
- Which regions drive the majority of sales?
- How do sales fluctuate over time?
- What payment methods do customers prefer?

This project simulates the work of a **Data Analyst in a retail company**, where the goal is to transform **raw sales data into actionable business insights**.

Using Python and data analysis techniques, we analyze **September sales data** to uncover meaningful patterns that can help businesses make smarter decisions.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

At the end of September, the management team wants to answer the following questions:

1. What is the **total revenue generated in September?**  
2. Which **products generated the highest sales?**  
3. Which **cities and areas contributed the most revenue?**  
4. How do **sales vary across different dates?**  
5. Which **payment methods are most commonly used?**

This project answers these questions through **data analysis and visualization**.

From the analysis, several important insights emerge:

✔ A small number of products generate a **large portion of total revenue**
